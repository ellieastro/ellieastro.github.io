Current Research

The focus of my PhD research has been an observational analysis of the multiple stellar populations in 30 Galactic Globular Clusters (GCs). I use a combination of Hubble Space Telescope photometry and ground-based photometry from the homogeneous catalogue provided by Stetson et al. (2019)
